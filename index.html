.about

As an award-winner director/cameraman, J. Wesley Jones has excelled is the craft of commercial filmmaking for over thirty years.

J. Wesley established himself as a director/cameraman with innovative in-camera special effects, saturated, mouth watering use of color, and the edgy photography that sets his style apart. He is an expert with liquids and high-speed photography, and he has produced so many pieces featuring beer, soda and other liquids that he earned the nickname, "The King of Splash" in Japan.
